Before You Apply

Everything You Need to Know

Please read this guide carefully before beginning your application. Being well-prepared will make the process smoother.

Estimated time: 45–90 minutes for Part 1 (personal data & documents). You may save your progress and return at any time. Part 2 takes approximately 15 minutes and must be completed in one session.

Step 1 — Create Account

Register with your email. A 6-digit verification code will be sent to your inbox to activate your account.

Step 2 — Complete Your Data

Fill in your personal information, education background, and programme preferences. Save progress anytime.

Step 3 — Upload Documents

Upload all required documents in PDF format (max 5 MB each). Ensure all files are clear and legible.

Step 4 — Complete Questionnaire

Answer the Student Profile Questionnaire — a brief self-reflection to help us understand you as a prospective student.

Documents to Prepare

  • Valid passport (min. 18 months validity from application date)
  • Passport-size photo — white background, JPG or PNG
  • High school diploma + certified sworn translation
  • Final school transcript + certified sworn translation
  • Teacher recommendation letter (use UMB official template)
  • Financial support declaration — signed & stamped by parent/sponsor
  • Health certificate from a certified hospital or clinic
  • No-work declaration (use UMB official template)
  • Language certificate (TOEFL/IELTS) — if available, not mandatory
  • Scholarship letter — if applying with a scholarship

Parent / Guardian Data

If you have a legal guardian other than your parents, please complete the fields below. Otherwise leave blank.

Emergency Contact in Indonesia

A person residing in Indonesia who can be reached on your behalf in an emergency — such as a relative, community member, or sponsor representative. If you do not have one yet, leave blank and update after arrival.

Educational Background

State your final cumulative grade and the scale used — e.g. "3.8 out of 4.0" or "85 out of 100".

Language Proficiency

Not mandatory, but submitting a language certificate may strengthen your application.
